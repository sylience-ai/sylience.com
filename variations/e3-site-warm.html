About

Sylience is a public benefit corporation whose mission is to improve reasoning and enable broad participation in decision-making, foster human connection, and enhance human agency.

We're building Chord, the platform for orchestrated communication.

Our Approach

We believe that better tools for communication lead to better decisions. Chord helps teams align on complex problems by structuring conversations around evidence and reasoning rather than rhetoric.
