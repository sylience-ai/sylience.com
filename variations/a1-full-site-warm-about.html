About Sylience

Sylience is a public benefit corporation whose mission is to improve reasoning and enable broad participation in decision-making, foster human connection, and enhance human agency.

Our Mission

We believe that better reasoning leads to better decisions, and better decisions lead to better outcomes for everyone. Our work focuses on building tools that help humans think more clearly, communicate more effectively, and collaborate more productively.

What We're Building

Sylience is the company behind Chord, the platform for orchestrated communication. Chord helps teams make better decisions, faster, by structuring conversations and surfacing insights that would otherwise be lost.

Our Values

We're committed to building technology that augments human capability rather than replacing it. We believe in transparency, thoughtful design, and putting people first.
