About

Sylience is a public benefit corporation whose mission is to improve reasoning and enable broad participation in decision-making, foster human connection, and enhance human agency.

Our Mission

We believe that better tools for collective reasoning lead to better outcomes for everyone. Our work focuses on building technology that helps people think together more effectively.

What We're Building

Chord is a platform for orchestrated communication. It helps teams and organizations make better decisions by structuring conversations around evidence and reasoning.

Why "Sylience"?

The name reflects our belief that the best thinking often happens in quiet, reflective spaces. We're building tools that create room for thoughtful consideration amid the noise of modern communication.
